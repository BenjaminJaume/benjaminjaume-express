Self Driving Car Project

This is one of my earliest project.

This all started in 2010. It was at the time when I was learning programming at school with an Arduino board. I imagined at the time that the possibilities of things to do where infinite ! And it was time for me to experiment something. My best friend, Axel was studying micro-component and electronic at a deeper level than me and he had already a good knowledge of how electronic component work. So we both agreed on starting a project together, and thought about the Self Driving Car! After days and days of welding, thinking, drinking beers and programming in a small garage, we actually made it! Unfortunately, I do not have any pictures / program left to this day
